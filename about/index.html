Hacettepe Undergraduate American Studies Journal

Epitaph was launched by a group of Hacettepe students who endeavored to provide a venue to further the collaboration between undergraduate American Studies institutions. Each year the members of Epitaph have worked to expand their readership to students and faculty in programs involving the history, philosophy, literature of the United States.
